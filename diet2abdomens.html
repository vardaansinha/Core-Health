Diet: Abdomens/Chest (2)


Recommended diet:

1. Continue to have whole grains, 2-3 days a week. Oatmeal and quinoa are some of the many good options.

2. Consume nuts AND beans! These contain lots of protein, fiber, and healthy fats.

3. Fruits/vegetables should be had in AT LEAST 2-3 servings per day. These help to burn flab and make you more healthy!

4. Processed sugar should rarely be a part of your diet. Try for a cheat meal every 7 days.

5. For meats/protein, consume items like eggs, chicken, and fish.

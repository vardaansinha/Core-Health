Bicep curls


What's this?

Bicep curls target the "short head" and "long head" muscles on the arm. This exercise is very beneficial for increasing arm strength.


How do you do a bicep curl?

Hold a dumbbell in each hand with your arms on your sides. If you do not have dumbbells or weights for your bicep curls, feel free to use household items! Things like your laundry detergent bottle, a somewhat heavy can of food, or a jug of milk if you like, can also work. Keep your upper arms in the same place, and exhale as your "curl" the weight up to shoulder level while contracting your bicep muslces.

1 rep is when you perform one curling motion, up AND down.

Pushups


What's this?

Pushups are a common exercise for gaining arm and pectoral strength.


How do you do a pushup?

While lying on the floor, front-faced, position your arms shoulder-width apart. Try to lift your back up, STRAIGHT, and keep pushing yourself to the ground and up with your arms.

1 rep, or repetition, is when you push yourself back up and down, once.

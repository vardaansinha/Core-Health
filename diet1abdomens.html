Diet: Abdomens/Chest (1)


Recommended diet:

1. Continue to have lots of whole grains. Oatmeal and quinoa are some of the many good options.

2. Consume nuts AND beans! These contain lots of protein, fiber, and healthy fats.

3. Make sure you are regularly eating fruits/vegetables! For abs specifically, bell peppers are very beneficial.

4. Gradually continue to take out processed sugar (candy, chocolate, etc.) from diet. They are unhealthy, and if you want to be healthy, processed sugar isn't the way to go!

5. For meats/protein, consume items like eggs, chicken, and fish.

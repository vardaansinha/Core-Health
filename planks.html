Planks


What's this?

Planks are a common exercise for arm and core strength.


How do you do a plank?

Place your forearms on the floor, and put your elbows in a position aligned to your shoulders and arms. Your arms should be a little less than a shoulder-width apart, and keep your back STRAIGHT. Hold this position for however many seconds necessary.

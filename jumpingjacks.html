Jumping Jacks


What's this?

Jumping jacks are an exercise that target your leg muscles, and also add quality to your cardio workout.


How do you do a jumping jack?

First, start by keeping your legs together, with your arms at your sides. Then, jump and spread your legs apart, with your arms apart as well. Then, jump back to starting position.

1 rep, or repetition, is when you jump to the spread position AND jump back to starting position.

Leg Lifts


What's this?

Leg-lifts are very good for building your abdominal muscles.


How do you do a leg lift?

Lie flat on the floor with your legs stretched out in front of you, barely apart from each other. Your hands should also be flat on the floor, near your sides. Then, bend your knees, and raise your legs up into the air. Your thighs should be in a perpendicular position to your body. Keep straightening your legs until your feet are pointed straight up to the sky, and then slowly lower your legs to the point where they are barely above the ground.

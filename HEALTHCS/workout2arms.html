Workout: Arms (2)


Recommended workout:

You have had some exposure to arm exercise, and we will start somewhat slow.

3 sets for two days a week:
NOTE: Make sure to stretch first! Get your arms loose by doing a few arm rotations. When you are ready, start:

20 pushups


45 second plank


Tricep dips (10 reps)

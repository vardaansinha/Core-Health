Decline Pushups


What's this?

Decline pushups are a variation of the pushup, where your legs are at an elevated position, making the pushup slightly harder, helping your chest muscles.


How do you do a decline pushup?

Find an object that you can keep your legs elevated on, and then keep your arms and palms in the normal pushup position on the floor. Go through the normal pushup motion, and you will find it to be somewhat harder.

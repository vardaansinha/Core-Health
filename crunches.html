Crunches


What's this?

Crunches are an exercise that will help to tone your abdominal muscles.


How do you do a crunch?

Lie on your back, knees bent and feet on the floor, hip-width apart. Put both of your hands behind your head, but don't intertwine your fingers. Your elbows should be slightly out to the sides. Curl up and up so that your back, head, and neck are off the ground. Hold it for a few seconds, and go back down.

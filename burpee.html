Burpees


What's this?

Burpees are a full body exercise used for strength training. This exercise helps with your arm strength.


How do you do a burpee?

Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart. Jump up, and then squat down into a pushup position to do one pushup. Jump back up, and keep doing the same.

1 rep, or repetition, is when you jump down, go back down, and do one pushup.

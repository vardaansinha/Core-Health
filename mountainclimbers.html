Mountain Climbers


What's this?

Mountain climbers are a good exercise for core strength, and the abdomens/chest.


How do you do a mountain climber?

Get into a plank position, with your palms face down on the floor. Pull your right/left knee into your chest as far as you can, similar to a running motion while in position. Then, switch legs, while keeping your back straight, with a fast pace.

Diet: Cardio (3)


Recommended diet:

1. Make complex carbohydrates a part of your regular snacks, post and pre workout. These provide you with useful energy boosts!

2. Incorporate healthy fats into your meal. This can be through nuts, avocados, cheese, and olive oil.

3. Protein, protein, protein. Regularly have chicken and fish for meals, and for vegetarians, have fruit smoothies WITH protein powder supplements. Protein bars are also very effective. These also help to strengthen leg muscles.

4. Processed sugar should be completely cut out of your diet.

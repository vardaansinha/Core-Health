V-sits


What's this?

V-sits are an exercise that build abdominal and core strength.


How do you do a v-sit?

Sit on the ground, with your back flat on the ground and legs extended into the air, at a diagonal position. Then, lift your back off the ground, and touch your toes with your hands. You should feel the stretch in your abdominal muscles, and then go back to the original position.
